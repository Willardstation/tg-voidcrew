survey_scanner
/obj/machinery/survey_scanner

Survey scanner

A machine that generates research points, at the cost of power, instead of forcing people to waste their time standing idle with an item in their hand. Meant to be a stable way of generating points while encouraging moving to different planets Without the cost of doing the same thing on repeat (like dissections).
